A multimedia installation showing that our personalities and cultural tendencies lie within our clicks, likes, follows, views, swipes, and profiles. It's dance, code, and electronic music inspired the intricate ways that we curate our online selves. It's narcissistic. It's goofy. It's self aware. It's sad, because it points out that our own self-indulgent, attention whoring perpetuates harmful stereotypes, allows people to use our data to profile us around those stereotypes. Our clicks and keep us eternally wrapped up in our own bullshit.
